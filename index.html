Here are some places where I travelled last year...

The Longest Sea Beach

It is not only famous for its long natural sandy sea beach, but it is also famous for the amazing hospitality, fresh seafood, marine drive, kutubdia, Moheshkhali Island, and amazing St. Martin's Island. Mainly Cox's Bazar is famous for its longest natural sandy beach in the world.

Sajek

Sajek Valley is famous for its natural beauty and is also known as the Queen of Hills and Roof of Rangamati. Where the mind and soul get the feeling of heaven. There is no human noise but there is the melody of birds, the flow of fountains.For visiting sajek,the Rainy,Autumn,Late autumn are the best time.

Alutila-Cave-Khagrachari

Alutila-Cave is one of the most spectacular places with hills, forest, waterfalls and the life of aborigine in Bangladesh like Hajachora waterfall,Sajek Valley, Alutila Cave,Kanglang mount, Artisian well, Richhing waterfall etc. Traveler will find the natural wild beauty in Khagrachari.

Kaptai Lake

Kaptai Lake is one of the most important freshwater which is the largest man-made freshwater resource in the South-East Asia as well as in Bangladesh. The Kaptai Lake was created by damming the river Karnaphuli near Kaptai of Rangamati district in 1961.It contributes to produce significant quantity of freshwater fishes, navigation, flood control and agriculture.

Central Railway Building (CRB)

Central Railway Building (CRB) is situated in the scenic hilly area of Chittagong, Bangladesh. It is the governing office for the general manager of Bangladesh Railway. The building was completed in 1872, it is one of the oldest buildings of the port city.

Polwel Park & Cottage

Polwel Park & Cottage is a modern, elegant and secured luxury resort situated beside Kaptai lake which is managed by Rangamati District Police.It provides the mesmerizing beauty of Rangamati Hill Tracts, panoramic Kaptai lake views, beach, rich culture and history of tribal people. Polwel Park & Cottage is the best place in Rangamati which provides you extraordinary comfort, luxury and services.
